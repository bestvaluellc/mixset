The playlist app for people who actually edit playlists.

MixSet is built around the part Apple Music does not focus on enough: shaping a playlist until it fits your moment.

✨

Describe a moment

Type what you are doing — a walk, drive, workout, cookout, focus session, or quiet morning — and MixSet starts a playlist draft.

🎧

Tuned to your taste

MixSet uses Apple Music signals to lean into artists and genres you already like, with genre chips when you want more control.

▶️

Preview before saving

Play quick previews from the draft list so you can catch songs that do not belong before they hit your library.

✅

Keep or remove

Use checkmarks to remove songs you are tired of, songs that miss the mood, or songs you simply do not want today.

🟡

Fill the Gaps

If you remove too many songs, MixSet adds fresh replacements and highlights the new gaps so you can review them quickly.

🔁

Make fresh playlists

Save your playlist recipes in My Sets, then create a new Apple Music playlist when a favorite walk, drive, or workout set gets stale.

Review every pick

Do not save a playlist blind.

Preview songs, read why a song fits, keep the good stuff, and remove anything that breaks the mood. MixSet helps you shape one until you are happy.

Keep it fresh

When a playlist gets old, make a new version.

Your saved sets remember the original moment and playlist recipe. Tap a saved set to make a new Apple Music playlist with different songs. Your old playlists stay unchanged.

How MixSet works

A simple flow for creating Apple Music playlists that fit what you are actually doing.

1

Describe

Enter a moment like “Morning walk,” “Road trip,” or “Backyard BBQ.”

2

Choose length

Pick how long the playlist should be, from a short session to a long drive.

3

Review

Preview songs, uncheck misses, and use Fill the Gaps when the list gets short.

4

Save

Save the finished playlist directly to your Apple Music library.
